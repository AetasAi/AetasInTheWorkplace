Why Aetas ITW is different

Not an EAP. Not a benefits platform. A business performance intervention.

Most financial wellbeing solutions are designed for large corporate employers. They rely on broad platforms, generic content, and the assumption that employees will engage independently. In practice, uptake is low, impact is hard to measure, and the business is left wondering whether any of it made a difference. In a smaller organisation, the stakes are simply higher.

01

The Review comes first — always

Nothing is introduced without first understanding your organisation, your workforce, and where pressure is actually showing up. No two programmes look the same.

02

Commercially grounded, not sentiment-led

Every recommendation has a commercial rationale. If the numbers do not justify it, we say so. Fees are presented alongside the savings we identify, so you can see the net position.

03

Managed on your behalf throughout

Aetas coordinates delivery, handles employee communications, and oversees implementation. You retain full visibility without being required to manage providers yourself.

04

Built for organisations where every employee matters

One disengaged person in a 30-person team represents more than three percent of your entire workforce. The margin for quiet underperformance is small. The return on addressing it is proportionally large.
