The challenge

The performance gaps most organisations recognise but rarely measure

The challenges are consistent across sectors. The commercial cost is rarely quantified. That is where Aetas Performance starts.

📋

Absence and presenteeism

UK businesses lose an estimated £51 billion per year to stress-related absence and presenteeism. For a 50-person business, that is a material number , and almost none of it appears on a management report.

👥

Recruitment and retention

Replacing a skilled employee costs between 50 and 200 per cent of their annual salary. A 15% turnover rate in a 60-person business means losing nine people a year. Most organisations have never calculated the actual invoice.

🌐

Benefits spend with no return

The average UK employer spends over £800 per employee per year on benefits. In most organisations, roughly 15% is unused or poorly understood. That is £12,000 wasted annually in a 100-person business.

📈

Leadership and management gaps

Poor management is the single biggest driver of employee disengagement. Every hour a senior person spends managing a people problem that a better-developed manager could handle is an hour not spent on the business.

👜

Culture that doesn't scale

What worked at 20 people rarely works at 80. Culture gaps become visible as recruitment problems, rising turnover, and declining engagement scores , by which point they are expensive to fix.

🎯

Employer brand falling behind

In a competitive labour market, the quality of candidates you attract reflects how your organisation is perceived. Organisations that invest in their people demonstrably attract better talent and retain them longer.
