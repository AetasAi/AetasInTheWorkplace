**Domain:** https://workplace.aetas-wealth.com/

**Updated:** July 2026

## Purpose

Workplace performance consultancy for SME employers. Helps organisations improve business performance through financial wellbeing, employee benefits review and strategic workplace planning. Entry point: no-cost Workplace Performance Audit.

## Key people

- Matthew Steiner, Managing Director and founder. matthew.steiner@aetas-wealth.com
- Daniel Cottam, Financial Planner. Delivers employee workshops and one-to-one guidance.

## Core framework

ROEI (Return on Employee Investment) - measures and improves the commercial return on total people investment across salary, pension, benefits, training, wellbeing and management time. Five stages: Measure, Diagnose, Recommend, Implement, Measure Again.
