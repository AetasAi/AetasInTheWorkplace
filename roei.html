What is ROEI?

Your people are your largest investment. Most businesses never measure the return.

Every employer invests in their workforce. Salaries, pensions, benefits, recruitment, training, leadership development, wellbeing. These commitments are significant and ongoing. Yet in most organisations, nobody is actively measuring whether those investments are generating the greatest possible return in performance, productivity and retention.

Aetas Performance changes that. Financial wellbeing is one lever. Leadership, employee benefits, culture, engagement, technology and commercial improvement are others. Together, these are the drivers that move employee performance, and through it, business performance. ROEI is the framework that measures the whole picture.

The ROEI Framework

Three stages. One repeatable method.

Every Aetas Performance engagement follows the same logic. It turns investment in people into measurable, documented business outcomes.

Stage 1

Measure

Understand how effectively your current investment in people is performing, and where value is being lost.

  • ✔Leadership capability and gaps
  • ✔Employee benefits and pension engagement
  • ✔Financial wellbeing indicators
  • ✔Culture and engagement signals
  • ✔Avoidable turnover and absence
  • ✔Commercial performance baseline

Stage 2

Improve

Fix the cause with the right drivers, each introduced only with a clear commercial rationale, and delivered on your behalf.

  • ✔Leadership development and training
  • ✔Financial wellbeing programmes
  • ✔Employee benefits and pension restructure
  • ✔Culture and engagement initiatives
  • ✔Delivery, communications and provider oversight handled for you

Stage 3

Prove

Re-measure and document the return. ROEI is a continuous cycle, not a one-off exercise.

  • ✔Engagement and retention metrics
  • ✔Benefits utilisation and financial confidence
  • ✔Absence and productivity data
  • ✔Organisational performance review
